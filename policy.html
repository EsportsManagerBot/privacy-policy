Privacy Policy for Esports Manager Bot

1. Introduction

Welcome to Esports Manager, a Discord bot designed to manage and facilitate esports tournaments. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect user data when using the bot.

2. Information We Collect

  • User IDs: Used to identify players and manage tournament participation.
  • Server IDs & Channel IDs: Used to operate within designated servers and manage tournament-related channels.
  • Tournament Data: Includes team names, tournament structures, and match results.

We do not collect personal data such as email addresses, phone numbers, or any sensitive personal information.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To manage tournament registrations, teams, and brackets.
  • To send tournament updates and notifications.
  • To provide tournament history and analytics.

4. Data Sharing & Protection

We do not sell, trade, or share user data with third parties. All stored data is secured and only accessible by authorized bot administrators.

5. User Rights

Users can request the deletion of their tournament-related data by contacting the bot administrators. Deleting data may impact participation in ongoing tournaments.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be communicated via the bot or an official announcement.

7. Contact

For questions regarding this policy, please contact the bot administrators.
